The History of cats

The history of cats streches back Thousands of years. The earlisest domestication of cats started around 9,000 years ago, in modern day Eygpt, Iraq and Turkey. by around 4,000 BCE, cats were highly valued for their hunting abilities.Killing cats at that time was considered a serious crime, and many cats were mummified alongside their owners.

Fun Facts About Cats

  1. Wisker sensitivity: cats whiskers are highly sensitive and can detect even the slightest changes in their surroundings.
  2. They sweat Through Their Paws: Cats dont sweat like humans. They only have sweat glands on their paw pads, which is why you might notice their paw prints when they are nervous.
  3. A Cat's Nose is Unique: Just like humans fingerprints every cats nose print is unique.
